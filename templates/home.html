About the Project

This project uses advanced machine learning algorithms to classify sleep disorders such as insomnia and sleep apnea. With the help of our model, we aim to improve the accuracy and efficiency of sleep disorder diagnosis.

Key Features:

  • Accurate Predictions: Classifies various sleep disorders with an accuracy surpassing 92%.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Uses a dataset with 1,500 records and 13 features, including sleep patterns, lifestyle, and health indicators.
  • Multiple Algorithms: Implements a range of machine learning models such as Decision Tree, Support Vector Machine, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and Artificial Neural Networks.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy to use for anyone interested in understanding sleep-related issues.

How It Works:

  • Data Preprocessing: We clean and normalize the data, ensuring it's ready for analysis.
  • Feature Extraction: Important features like blood pressure, heart rate, and sleep duration are carefully extracted and used.
  • Machine Learning Models: Multiple models are trained and tested to ensure accurate predictions.
  • Prediction: Based on your inputs, the model predicts whether you might be at risk for certain sleep disorders.

Why Sleep Disorders Matter:

Sleep disorders affect millions worldwide, leading to poor health, productivity issues, and overall quality of life. Early detection and proper diagnosis are key to improving your sleep health.
